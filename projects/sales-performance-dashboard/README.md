# Sales Performance Dashboard

## Business Problem
A retail or telecom company needs a clear view of monthly sales performance, product trends and revenue opportunities. The goal is to identify growth areas, underperforming products and actionable insights for sales teams.

## Tools
- Power BI
- Excel

## Approach
- Data cleaning and preparation in Excel
- Import into Power BI
- KPI creation (Total Sales, Avg Order Value, Conversion Rate)
- Product performance analysis
- Monthly trends and forecasting
- Customer-level revenue insights

## Key Insights (example placeholders)
- Product category X generates 45% of total revenue.
- Sales drop by 12% in Q2 due to seasonality.
- Top 10% customers contribute 60% of revenue.

## Business Impact (hypothetical scenario)
By focusing on high-value customers and promoting top-performing categories, revenue could increase by 8–12% in the next quarter.
