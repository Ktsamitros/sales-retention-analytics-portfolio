# Customer Transactions & Revenue Dashboard (2024–2025)
### Power BI Project — Synthetic Randomly Generated Dataset

## 📌 Overview
This project presents a complete **Power BI dashboard** analyzing customer transactions and revenue performance for the years **2024–2025**.  
All data used in this project is **100% synthetic and randomly generated** for educational and portfolio purposes.  
No real customer, financial, or business information is included.

The dashboard provides a clear, business‑oriented view of revenue trends, product performance, regional distribution, and sales activity.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### **1. Monthly Revenue Trend**
A chronological bar chart showing revenue evolution across 24 months.

### **2. Revenue by Sales Representative**
Comparison of total revenue generated by each sales rep.

### **3. Revenue by Region**
Performance breakdown across Attica, Greek Islands, Northern Greece, and Southern Greece.

### **4. Revenue by Product Type (Pie Chart)**
Contribution of Mobile, Internet/Fiber, and TV products.

### **5. Average Revenue per Product Type (ARPT)**
A premium insight showing revenue quality per product category using:
ARPT = DIVIDE([Total Revenue], [Total Transactions])
