# Customer Churn Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Business Problem
A telecom company experiences increased customer churn after the 3rd month of subscription.  
The goal is to identify churn drivers, understand customer behavior, and propose actions to reduce attrition.

---

## 🛠 Tools Used
- Power BI (data modeling, DAX, dashboard design)
- Excel (data cleaning & preparation)

---

## 📊 Approach
- Data cleaning & preprocessing in Excel  
- Import into Power BI  
- Creation of calculated columns (Tenure, Tenure Groups)  
- DAX measures for churn KPIs  
- Churn trend analysis  
- Churn drivers analysis (usage, complaints, billing issues)  
- Customer segmentation (high‑risk groups)

---

## 📈 Key Insights
- Customers with 2+ complaints show **3× higher churn probability**.  
- Low usage in the first 10 days is a strong churn predictor.  
- Customers with billing issues churn significantly more.  
- Short‑tenure customers (0–6 months) have the highest churn rate.  
- Consumer segment shows slightly higher churn than Business.

---

## 💼 Business Impact
By targeting high‑risk customers with proactive retention offers, churn could be reduced by **10–15%**, resulting in significant revenue savings.
