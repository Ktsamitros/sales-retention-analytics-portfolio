# Churn & Retention Dashboard

## Business Problem
A telecom company experiences increased customer churn after the 3rd month of subscription. The goal is to identify churn drivers and propose actions to reduce customer attrition.

## Tools
- Power BI
- Excel

## Approach
- Data cleaning and preparation in Excel
- Import into Power BI
- Cohort analysis
- Churn drivers analysis (complaints, usage, billing)
- Segmentation of high-risk customers

## Key Insights (example placeholders)
- Customers with 2+ complaints have 3x higher churn probability.
- Low usage in the first 10 days is a strong churn predictor.

## Business Impact (hypothetical scenario)
By targeting high-risk customers with a retention offer, churn could be reduced by ~10–15%, leading to significant revenue savings.
